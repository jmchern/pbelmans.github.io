  • Andrea Ricolfi: Introduction to enumerative geometry are a beautiful set of lecture notes (in progress) on enumerative geometry. If the link is broken, it should be possible to find the newest version via Andrea's webpage.

  • Igor Burban, Yuriy Drozd: Morita theory for non-commutative noetherian schemes amongst other things proves an noncommutative version of Gabriel's reconstruction theorem: the central subscheme is determined purely in terms of the abelian category of quasicoherent sheaves. Cool stuff!

  • Ciro Ciliberto, Mikhail Zaidenberg: Lines, conics, and all that gives a big literature overview of Fano schemes of lines, conics and all that (sic). 481 references!
