In Bonn

Joint with Catharina Stroppel and Paul Wedrich I organise the Oberseminar Representation Theory at the University of Bonn. It takes place in 1.008, on Friday, from 12h15 to 13h15.

For other seminars related to the algebras and representation theory group, see the group's website.

In Antwerp

I used to organise ANAGRAMS, a weekly student seminar at the University of Antwerp, centered around the topics of (noncommutative) algebra and algebraic geometry. Please take a look at the seminar's webpage for more information.

I also used to co-organise the weekly departmental seminar, for which there is a mailing list.
